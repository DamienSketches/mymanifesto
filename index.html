A Sketch of a Manifesto

Started: November.05.20XX

  

Art is all I know. It is all I can do.....

After all, school was and still is a struggle. Ever since I was young,

I struggled immensely in school- disabilities, mental illnesses, and- being bullied every day;

does not make the learning experience easy. Especially having no help

and the ability to get diagnosed (to know yourself better) and understand you can't control these things.

I got better in other areas of schooling by having to try so much

longer and harder than my other peers leading to sobbing fits.

Art is my safe place, freedom, and outlet for my excessive daydreams and frustrations.

Drafting and Sketching my way through life to coping skills.

That freedom that sketching gives me is like a medicine that makes me giggle and smile.

But, art is not always positive for me, I struggle with my art.

I have to think very intentionally and keep in mind all the education

I got since coming to college to keep my finished art looking the way it does;

even if I do it in my own "Damien" way of understanding.

From elementary to high school- I was bullied for many reasons, one of them; being my art.

It created a toxic view of myself as an artist and the art I produced.

I constantly got compared to my friends saying- my art was dog shit

compared to theirs, among other things, even though I was trying to learn.

That bullying and self-view still have their claws on me today.

I work myself to death just trying to get a sliver of approval

and hide my work from the public/internet, terrified to get those same comments.

I have to force myself to like my art at times- while trying not to

throw away a piece I work so hard on every time.

College was the first time I got meaningful praise, compliments, and admiration

for my work. It always makes me sob when I get out of class.

The reasoning is as they mean so much to me. I feel un-deserving every time I get

them as I struggle with imposter syndrome. I am slowly trying to work on my issues,

and it has allowed me to be better at art and genuinely like my work regardless.

I started working for myself and sketching to my heart's content to feel freedom again.

I want- no, I need to show the world my characters and story.

I hope to inspire the people who see it too.

I want to be meaningful and silly: Also, I want emotions to be felt.

All I have to do is sketch until it works..

  

Finished: December.06.20XX
